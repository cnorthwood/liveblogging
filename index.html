Live Blogging for WordPress

The most popular live blogging plugin for WordPress

Live Blogging for WordPress allows for WordPress blogs to embed live blogs within your posts, which can be updated without users ever having to refresh the page. Perfect for blogging events.

Tried and tested, live blogging for WordPress is capable of scaling up to even the biggest live blogs. The simple and effective approach works straight out of the box, or allows you to customise the blog to your desire.
